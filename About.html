About Us

The Center began offering services to the local Community in 1965 when the parish priest made a plea for local women to volunteer a few hours a week to help needy families in the area. We extended our serices to helping families from the travelling community and local senior citizens. Over the years both the services and the facilies have improved.

The Walkinstown Social Service Center was built in the 1970's and remains in the grounds of the sisters of Charity Convent Walkinstown, As the building has evolved so too have the Services
